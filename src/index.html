Who We Are

Your Exclusive Build Ltd are fully insured run by Michael and Kerrin and have 17 years experience in the building industry. We pride ourselves in the quality of our workmanship, from communicating with our customers to the build itself.

Unlike other builders, we will be on site 95% of the job, as we do not contract the work out to sub contractors but carry out the work ourselves, and therefore are in full control of the quality of work.

We also project manage to save you the time and stress and therefore won't have any organising to do, resulting in a much more efficient job. We have a close team of plumbers and electricians to make sure the job runs as smoothly as possible.
